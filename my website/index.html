My name is Roman Safonov; good day. I'm a 22-year-old Lithuanian native who was born in
Klaipeda. I'm really passionate about a variety of interests. Skateboarding, programming,
playing video games, and having meaningful conversations with people from various cultures and countries
are a few of them. But my most cherished and persistent pastime is singing and playing the guitar.
When I was 13 years old,I started taking official lessons with a music teacher and did so for a whole year.
After that, I started a 10-year adventure in which I learned and mastered cover songs on the guitar and sang them to life.
This pairing of singing and guitar playing has been a consistent source of happiness and artistic expression.

I had chances to show off my musical abilities during my formative years in a variety of contexts, including school,
high school events, summer camps, and even once on a public bus. My love for performing covers of songs that
were very dear to me usually took center stage during these performances. I always carried my trusty guitar with
me when I entered a performing area or a stage. The guitar was more than simply a tool for me; it was also my compass,
allowing me to direct my attention on the music and calming my nerves before performances. The photo up top was taken
during one of these special occasions when I was 16 and performing at a summer camp.
