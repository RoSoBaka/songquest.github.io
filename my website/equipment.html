I predominantly gravitate towards acoustic guitars for playing cover songs due to their portability and convenience.
However, my longstanding desire has been to delve into the realm of electric guitars, particularly within the context
of a band. The challenge lies in the inherent difficulty of assembling a group of like-minded individuals who share
the same musical passion. To make the transition smoother, I've also taken to utilizing classical guitars, notable
for their plastic strings as opposed to metal ones. The advantage of classical guitars lies in their reduced likelihood
of causing discomfort or pain in the fingers. It was with this consideration in mind that I initiated my musical journey
with classical guitar, aiming to progress further.

I primarily utilize the Blue Yeti microphone for recording my cover songs. To ensure the highest sound quality,
I meticulously seek out quiet environments, free from any white noise interference. When it comes to recording
my electric guitar, I rely on an amplifier; it's the essential link that transforms mere string noise into a harmonious
and appealing sound, ensuring the instrument's true sonic potential is realized.
